About the QUBE Project

QUBE is a student-led initiative exploring the fascinating world of quantum computing. We aim to make quantum education more accessible through visual tools, simulators, and tutorials.

Our team has built a quantum simulator using Java that allows users to test basic algorithms like Shor's and Grover's. With QUBE, we want to spark curiosity and lower the barrier to entry into quantum science.
