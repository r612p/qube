About the QUBE Project

QUBE is a student-led initiative designed to make quantum computing easier to understand and explore. By building interactive tools and simulators, we aim to spark curiosity and give learners a hands-on way to experiment with quantum concepts.

As highschoolers, we noticed a gap in the education system where quantum computing, a subject that can be intuitive to learn the basics of, isn't included. The only option for students who are interested in quantum computing is to learn from online sources or wait until college. We also noticed that quantum computing comes off as a very complex subject that many students are afraid to dive into. We aim to bridge this gap and facilitate quantum learning to youth and bring interest to the next generation; our generation of quantum engineering and computing.

Our Goals

  • Develop clear and intuitive quantum visualizations
  • Release an open-source quantum simulator
  • Build community-driven learning modules and workshops
